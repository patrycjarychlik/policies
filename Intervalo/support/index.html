Support

Customer support and common questions for Intervalo

If you need help with Intervalo, send a message to intervalo.app@gmail.com.

Best way to get help

Include the device model, iOS version, and a short description of the issue. If the app shows an error, include the exact wording.

Question

How do I report a bug?

Email a short description of what happened, what was expected, and the steps that led to the issue.

Question

How do I delete my data?

Intervalo stores data only on the device. Removing the app deletes the locally stored app data.

Question

I purchased a subscription or feature and need billing help.

Purchases are managed by Apple. Billing, refunds, and subscription management are handled through the App Store account settings.

Question

How quickly can I expect a reply?

Support requests are answered by email as soon as possible. Response time can vary depending on volume and weekends.
