Intervalo does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal information. Everything stays on your device.

Overview

This Privacy Policy describes how Intervalo ("the App", "we", "our") handles information when you use our iOS application. We are committed to protecting your privacy. This policy applies to all versions of Intervalo available on the Apple App Store.

Information We Collect

We do not collect any personal information. The App functions entirely on your device and does not transmit any data to us or any third party. Specifically, we do not collect:

  • Name, email address, or any identifiers
  • Device identifiers or advertising identifiers
  • Location data
  • Health or fitness data
  • Usage data, analytics, or diagnostics
  • Contacts, photos, or media
  • Financial or payment information

Data Storage

All app settings, timers, and preferences you create within Intervalo are stored locally on your device using Apple's standard on-device storage. This data never leaves your device and is fully under your control. Deleting the app removes all associated data.

Third-Party Services

Intervalo does not integrate any third-party analytics, advertising, crash-reporting SDKs, or tracking frameworks. We do not use Google Analytics, Firebase, Amplitude, or any similar service.

The App is distributed via the Apple App Store. Apple may collect certain data as part of operating the App Store platform, subject to Apple's Privacy Policy.

Children's Privacy

Intervalo does not collect any data from any users, including children under the age of 13. The App is fully compliant with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA) and Apple's guidelines for apps available to children.

Your Rights

Because we collect no personal data, there is nothing to access, correct, export, or delete on our end. All data the App stores resides locally on your device and can be removed at any time by deleting the App.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated effective date. We encourage you to review this policy periodically. Continued use of the App after any changes constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.
