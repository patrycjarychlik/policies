Overview

This Privacy Policy describes how Intervalo ("the App", "we", "our") handles information when you use our iOS application. We are strongly committed to your privacy. The App is designed to function entirely on your device and does not collect personal data.

This policy applies to all versions of Intervalo distributed via the Apple App Store.

Data We Do Not Collect

We do not collect, transmit, or store on our servers any of the following:

  • Name, email address, or any personal identifiers
  • Device identifiers or advertising identifiers (IDFA)
  • Location data
  • Health or fitness data
  • Contacts, photos, or media
  • Microphone or camera input
  • Crash reports or usage analytics
  • Financial or payment information

Data Stored on Your Device

The App saves the following data locally on your device only, using Apple's standard on-device storage (AsyncStorage). This data is never transmitted to us or any third party.

Data Purpose Stored
Workout definitions Saves your custom interval timers Device only
Session history Last 50 completed workout sessions (date, duration, phase count) Device only
Preferences Sound theme, haptics, display settings, accent color Device only
Subscription status Whether Pro is active (granted by Apple; no payment details stored) Device only

Deleting the App removes all of this data from your device permanently.

Audio

The App plays audio cues (interval sounds) during workouts using your device's speaker. Audio is used for playback only — the App never accesses the microphone and does not record audio.

In-App Purchases

Intervalo offers an optional Pro subscription sold through the Apple App Store. All purchase transactions are handled entirely by Apple — we never receive, process, or store your payment information, Apple ID, or billing details.

When you purchase or restore a subscription, Apple communicates the purchase result to the App. The App stores only a local flag indicating whether Pro access is active. For details on how Apple handles your purchase data, see Apple's Privacy Policy.

External Content

Workout cover images are loaded from Unsplash (unsplash.com), a third-party image CDN. When the App displays a workout card, your device fetches the image directly from Unsplash's servers. Unsplash may log standard request metadata (IP address, timestamp) per their own privacy policy. No personal data from within the App is sent to Unsplash.

Third-Party SDKs & Analytics

The App does not integrate any analytics, advertising, crash-reporting, or tracking SDKs. Specifically, the App does not use:

  • Google Analytics, Firebase, or any Google SDK
  • Amplitude, Mixpanel, Segment, or similar analytics
  • Sentry, Bugsnag, Crashlytics, or crash-reporting tools
  • Any advertising network or ad SDK

Children's Privacy

The App does not collect data from any users, including children under 13. It is compliant with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA) and Apple's guidelines for apps available to younger audiences.

Your Rights

Because we do not collect personal data on our servers, there is no data for us to provide, correct, or delete on your behalf. All data the App stores resides locally on your device and is fully under your control — you can delete it at any time by removing the App.

If you have questions about the purchase data Apple holds, please refer to Apple's privacy tools at privacy.apple.com.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated effective date. Continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
